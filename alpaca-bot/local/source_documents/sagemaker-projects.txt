<h1>Automate MLOps with SageMaker Projects<a name="sagemaker-projects"></a></h1>

<p>Create end-to-end ML solutions with CI/CD by using SageMaker projects. </p>

<p>Use SageMaker projects to create an MLOps solution to orchestrate and manage:
+ Building custom images for processing, training, and inference
+ Data preparation and feature engineering
+ Training models
+ Evaluating models
+ Deploying models
+ Monitoring and updating models</p>
