<h1>sagemaker-notebook-no-direct-internet-access<a name="sagemaker-notebook-no-direct-internet-access"></a></h1>

<p>Checks whether direct internet access is disabled for an Amazon SageMaker notebook instance. The rule is NON_COMPLIANT if Amazon SageMaker notebook instances are internet-enabled. </p>

<p><strong>Identifier:</strong> SAGEMAKER_NOTEBOOK_NO_DIRECT_INTERNET_ACCESS</p>

<p><strong>Trigger type:</strong> Periodic</p>

<p><strong>AWS Region:</strong> All supported AWS regions except Asia Pacific (Jakarta), Africa (Cape Town), Middle East (UAE), Asia Pacific (Hyderabad), Asia Pacific (Osaka), Asia Pacific (Melbourne), Europe (Milan), AWS GovCloud (US-East), Europe (Spain), Europe (Zurich) Region</p>

<p><strong>Parameters:</strong></p>

<p>None  </p>
